Missed Opportunities
in the US-Iran Relationship

The relationship between the United States and Iran has been troubled since the 1979 revolution in Iran, and now is caught in a continuing spiral of suspicions and accusations. This mutual hostility has many causes in real events going back to the early 1950s and in the national narratives that have grown over the decades. Remarkably, there have also been opportunities to improve the relationship and put the misunderstandings and tensions aside. Most of these opportunities have been squandered, however, and now seem irretrievable. full text
